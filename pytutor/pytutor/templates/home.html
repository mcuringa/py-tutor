PyTutor is social tutoring software to help you learn the basics of programming in Python. We believe in community-supported learning and collaboration when learning to code.
We bring in features from social networks to make it easy to ask your friends for a little help if you get stuck.
Our wiki-like question editor makes it quick and easy to write many high-quality questions. After you answer some questions and get a sense for PyTutor, just click "new question" and help us grow.
PyTutur is under active development, but is in a private beta. If you would like to try it out, send us an email at mcuringa@adelphi.edu and we will let you know when you can get started.
